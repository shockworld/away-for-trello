Small, focused, and privacy-friendly

👀

Visible where work happens

Show the notice on every card or only on cards assigned to the person who is away.

📅

Expires automatically

The notice stops showing when the configured return date arrives.

🔒

No external database

Your notice stays in Trello's shared board storage. We run no backend or analytics.

Required Trello capabilities

Enable all four capabilities in the Trello app administration portal. Away for Trello does not require REST API authorization or access to an external account.

🔘

board-buttons

Shows the current status and opens the full notice from the board header.

🏷️

card-badges

Displays the active vacation notice on the front of every card.

🪪

card-detail-badges

Keeps the notice visible when someone opens a card directly from a link.

⚙️

show-settings

Adds the configuration screen for creating and updating a notice.
